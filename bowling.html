Bowling is a very slay sport.

Tennis is a team sport, with typically 4 people on each team. Bowling is played in lanes in bowling alleies. Players roll a bowling ball down the lane and try to score points by knocking down bowling pins.

Points are scored according to how many of the 10 bowling pins they knocked down in two rolls.

There are many different plays in bowling, consisting of:
  • Strike: This occurs when the player knocks down all 10 pins in their first roll. This is awarded 10 points plus double points for their next two rolls.
  • Spare: This occurs when the player knocks down 10 pins in 2 rolls. This is also awarded 10 points plus double points on their next first roll
  • Normal scoring: This occurs when the player knocks down any number of pins during their two rolls. This is awarded the same number of points as pins they knocked down
  • Gutterball: This occurs when the ball is rolled into the gutters at the edge of the lane resulting in 0 points.

Common stratagies

  • Where to stand: Bowlers must stand behind the line at the edge of each lane and can be penalized for overstepping.
  • How to hold the ball: Bowlers should hold the ball putting their thumb in the bottom hole and two other fingers in the top holes.
  • How to roll the ball: Bowlers should step forward while moving their ball hand backward, then move their hand forward and release the ball.
