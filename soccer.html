Soccer is a very slay sport.

Soccer is a team sport where two teams compete for the most points scored during a 90 minute game.

  • Points are scored when players kick the ball into the opponents net
  • 11 players are on the field at any given time, typically consisting of:
  • 1 goal keeper -or goalie- who blocks balls using their body to prevent the opposing team from scoring points
  • 4 defenders who help the goal keeper by preventing players from kicking the ball towards the goal
  • 3 forwards who try to kick the ball down the field and into the opponents goal
  • 3 midfielders who help bring the ball back and forth between defenders and forwards
