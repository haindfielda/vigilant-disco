Tennis is a very slay sport.

Tennis is an individual and partner sport (1 vs. 1 or 2 vs. 2) played on a court with a net placed in the middle. The two teams volley the tennis ball back and forth using their rackets. They do this to score points.

"A game is won by the first player to have won at least four points in total and at least two points more than the opponent. The running score of each game is described in a manner peculiar to tennis: scores from zero to three points are described as "love", "15", "30", and "40", respectively."

There are many different stances in tennis, consisting of:

  • Open stance: Player stands with their feet apart facing parralel to the net, also the most common
  • Closed stance: Player's feet are placed with one closer and one farther from the net, also the most rare
  • Semi-open stance: A combination of closed and open stances, player's feet are diaconally placed facing the net
  • Neutral stance: Players feet are perpendicular to the net and in line with eachother, typically taught first

Common moves/penalties

  • Hand ball: this occurs when someones hand (excluding the goalie) touches the ball this typically results in a free kick
  • Out: this occurs when the ball crosses the field perimiter lines, resulting in a kick in or a throw in
  • Corner kick: this occurs when the ball is kicked out of bounds across the end line by the team who's goal is at that end
  • Goal kick: this occurs when the opponent kicks the ball out across the line of the opposing end of the field
  • Free kick: this occurs when a penalty is called and the team who was not penalized gets to kick the ball without player intervention
  • Penalty kick: this occurs when there is a penalty in the goalie box, resulting in the ball being placed a line in the box and kicked
